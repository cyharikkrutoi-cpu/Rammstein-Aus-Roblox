About

We Are Rammstein
Aus Roblox

Rammstein Aus Roblox is a band born inside Roblox, inspired by the legendary German metal group Rammstein. We came together with one goal — to bring the fire of Rammstein's live shows into the virtual world.

We perform live concerts inside Roblox, recreating the energy, the pyrotechnics, and the raw power of a real show. Whether you're a longtime Rammstein fan or just found us — you're welcome in the pit.

Join our Discord community to stay updated on upcoming concerts, behind-the-scenes content, and everything happening in our Roblox world.
